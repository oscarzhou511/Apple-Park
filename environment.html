Environment & Sustainability

A Commitment to Sustainability

Apple Park is one of the most energy-efficient buildings in the world. It is powered entirely by renewable energy, including a large rooftop solar installation and biogas fuel cells. The campus uses natural ventilation for most of the year, reducing the need for heating and air conditioning.

Pioneering Energy Solutions

The campus generates a significant portion of its energy on-site. The rooftop solar array is one of the largest corporate solar installations globally. Combined with fuel cells and other renewable sources, Apple Park operates on 100% renewable energy.

A Living Landscape

Over 9,000 trees have been planted on the campus, primarily native California species resistant to drought. The landscape design incorporates diverse flora and fauna, recreating a natural Californian savanna habitat. This not only enhances biodiversity but also provides beautiful, natural spaces for employees.
