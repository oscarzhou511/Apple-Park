Plan Your Visit to Apple Park

Experience the unique atmosphere of the Apple Park Visitors Center. Here's what you need to know to make the most of your trip.

Getting Here & Hours

Address

Apple Park Visitor Center
10600 N Tantau Ave
Cupertino, CA 95014

Hours of Operation

Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Sunday: 11:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Hours are subject to change. Please check Apple's official website for the latest information.

Parking & Transit

Complimentary underground parking is available on-site. The entrance is off Tantau Avenue. Public transit options are also available; check local transit schedules.

Visitor Tips

Make the Most of It

  • Allow at least 1-2 hours for your visit.
  • Photography is generally welcome in public areas.
  • The Visitors Center is wheelchair accessible.
  • Check for any special events or holiday hours before you go.
  • Wi-Fi is available for guests.

Nearby

Cupertino offers various dining and shopping options if you wish to extend your day in the area. The views from the rooftop terrace also give a sense of the surrounding green spaces that define Apple Park.
